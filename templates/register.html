    🛡️ GigShield Insurance Policy & Terms of Service

    Scroll to read...

    Effective Date: March 2026  |  Policy Version: 1.0

    1. COVERAGE SCOPE — WHAT IS COVERED

    GigShield provides parametric income protection insurance exclusively for platform-based delivery partners (hereinafter "Insured Partner"). This policy covers loss of income arising solely from the following qualifying external disruptions:

    • Environmental Disruptions: Rainfall exceeding 15mm/hour, temperatures exceeding 42°C or below 5°C, wind speeds exceeding 60km/h, visibility below 200m (fog/dust storms), severe flooding as reported by weather agencies.
    • Social Disruptions: Government-declared curfews, city-wide/zone-wide strikes affecting delivery access, sudden market or delivery zone closures mandated by public authority.
    • Platform Disruptions: Verified platform-wide app outages exceeding 3 consecutive hours as confirmed by Downdetector and/or partner platform API, directly preventing completion of deliveries.

    2. WHAT IS EXPLICITLY NOT COVERED

    The following are strictly excluded from this policy. GigShield will not pay for, and no claim will be processed relating to:

    • ❌ Health insurance, hospitalisation, medical expenses, or any illness-related claims
    • ❌ Accident insurance, personal injury, or disability
    • ❌ Vehicle repair, maintenance, fuel costs, or any vehicle-related expenses
    • ❌ Income loss due to personal choice, voluntary non-working days, or personal emergencies
    • ❌ Loss of earnings due to low order volume, competitive factors, or platform algorithmic changes
    • ❌ Any losses on days when the Insured Partner is verified to have completed one or more deliveries
    • ❌ Claims filed more than 72 hours after the qualifying disruption event

    3. PAYOUT STRUCTURE

    Payouts are classified as:

    • Full-Day Payout: Issued when qualifying disruption exceeds threshold levels and is sustained for ≥6 hours within the delivery operating window (06:00–22:00 IST).
    • Half-Day Payout: Issued when qualifying disruption is sustained for 3–5.99 hours within the delivery operating window.
    • Payout amounts are defined by your selected tier and are processed by 23:59 IST on the day of disruption, or within 24 hours.
    • Weekly records are maintained. A day where deliveries are confirmed via partner API data will have payout eligibility automatically voided, even if a disruption was logged.

    4. PREMIUM & BILLING

    • Premiums are billed on a weekly cycle, beginning from the date of subscription.
    • Non-payment will result in immediate suspension of coverage without refund of unused premium days.
    • Premiums may be revised with 14 days' notice. Continued use constitutes acceptance of revised rates.
    • No pro-rated refunds are available for partial weeks unless the policy is cancelled within 48 hours of the first payment.

    5. FRAUD PREVENTION & TERMINATION

    • GigShield employs AI-powered fraud detection. Claims flagged as fraudulent will be investigated and may result in immediate policy termination without refund.
    • GPS spoofing, false weather claim assertions, duplicate claims, or submitting claims on days where delivery activity is verified will constitute fraud.
    • GigShield reserves the right to share fraud findings with partner platforms and law enforcement.
    • A fraud score is calculated for every claim. A score ≥0.6 triggers manual review. A score ≥0.8 may result in automatic rejection.

    6. CLAIM VERIFICATION

    • All claims are verified against: (a) OpenWeatherMap data for the user's registered pincode; (b) Partner platform delivery logs; (c) Downdetector outage records; (d) Historical claim patterns.
    • GigShield's determination of disruption severity is final and binding. No appeals on weather data interpretation will be entertained.
    • Disputes may be submitted via the in-app report system and will be reviewed by our AI + human team within 7 business days.

    7. DATA PRIVACY & PARTNER SHARING

    • Delivery activity data is retrieved from your registered platform (Zomato, Swiggy, Amazon, etc.) via partner API integration. By accepting this policy, you consent to this data sharing.
    • Your personal data is stored securely in encrypted databases. It will not be sold to third parties for marketing purposes.
    • Claim data, fraud flags, and audit records may be shared with partner platforms and regulatory authorities as required by law.

    8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

    GigShield's total aggregate liability in any calendar month shall not exceed 10x the monthly premium paid by the Insured Partner. GigShield is not liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages of any kind.

    9. GOVERNING LAW

    This policy is governed by the laws of India. All disputes are subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Bengaluru, Karnataka.

    10. AMENDMENTS

    GigShield reserves the right to amend this policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated via email 14 days in advance. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of amendments.

    ⚠️ Important Reminder: This is income loss insurance ONLY. It does not cover health, vehicle repairs, accidents, or any personal injuries. By accepting, you confirm you have read and understood all terms above.
